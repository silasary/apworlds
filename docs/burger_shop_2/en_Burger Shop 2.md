# Burger Shop 2

## What does randomization do to this game?

All recipes, powerups, and stages are included in the item pool. Like Burger Shop, a majority of these
items are food recipes which make the game more difficult, and will be labeled as trap items in the
item pool.

- **Food items and recipes** — breakfast sandwiches, cereals, donuts, and hot drinks; lunch burgers,
  sides, drinks, shakes, sundaes, and snack trays; dinner plates, pasta, pizza, salads, soups, and
  desserts are locked until received. Customer orders update in real-time to only include unlocked
  recipes, so customers will never ask for something you haven't found yet.
- **Level access** is gated by **Progressive Burger Shop** keys. The first 15 levels are always
  accessible; each key received unlocks the next 15.
- **Customer items** — **Dog Biscuit**, **Shirt**, and **Menu** are included in the item pool for the Dog Lover, Shirtless Guy, and Old Chap.
- **Workstation**, **BurgerBot**, and **Lollipops** are items in the pool that must be found before
  they can be used.
- **Powerups** (Lollipop, Speed, Happy, Money, Freeze, Cone) are individual items. Each powerup
  will be available in all levels once it is unlocked.

## What is the goal when randomized?

Beat the final three Story Mode levels (118, 119, and 120). This requires all 7
Progressive Burger Shop 2 keys.

## Which items can be in another player's world?

- **Progressive Burger Shop** (7 copies) — level access keys
- **Customer items** — Dog Biscuit, Shirt, Menu
- **Food items** — all breakfast, lunch, and dinner recipes
- **Gameplay upgrades** — Workstation, BurgerBot, Lollipops, Lollipop Powerup, Speed Powerup, Happy
  Powerup, Money Powerup, Freeze Powerup, Cone Powerup
- **Filler** — Lucky Penny (and bonus recipes when the Bonus Recipes option is enabled)

## What does another world's item look like in Burger Shop 2?

The Archipelago client window shows a notification whenever an item is received. Customer orders
update automatically — starting or restarting a level will reflect any newly unlocked recipes.
Powerup changes take effect immediately without requiring a level restart.
