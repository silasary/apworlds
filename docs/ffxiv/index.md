# Final Fantasy XIV

Final Fantasy XIV is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) developed and published by Square Enix.
Explore the world of Eorzea, complete dungeons, trials, and FATEs to find multiworld items.
Items in FFXIV are: access to zones, increases to your soft level cap, and fishing tackle.
