# PlateUp

## What does randomization do to this game?

PlateUp's randomizer integrates with Archipelago to shuffle dish unlocks, appliance unlocks, day progression gates, and upgrade items into the multiworld pool. Day completions, stars, franchise milestones, achievements, and dish-specific checks all become location checks that send items to other players.

## What is the goal of PlateUp?

Depending on your settings, the goal is one of:

- **Franchise X Times**: Successfully franchise your restaurant a configurable number of times.
- **Complete X Days**: Survive and complete a configurable number of restaurant days across all runs.
- **Reach Day X With Dishes**: Reach a target day while having a minimum number of dishes active in your restaurant.

## What are location checks in PlateUp?

Location checks are in-game actions that send items to other players in the multiworld. Checks include:

- Completing each day of a run.
- Earning stars during a run.
- Completing franchise milestones.
- Losing a run.
- Unlocking and playing specific dishes (if dish checks are enabled).
- Triggering in-game achievements (if achievement checks are enabled).
- Meeting setting-based objectives (if setting checks are enabled).
- Rerolling
- Buying checks from pedestals

## What items can I receive?

Items you can receive from other players include:

- Dish unlock items, allowing you to open a restaurant with that dish.
- Appliance unlock items, adding appliances to your future shop rotations.
- Speed upgrades for your character and appliances.
- Day leases, required to progress past certain day intervals.
- Money cap increases, raising the maximum gold you can hold.
- Shop size increases, expanding your daily appliance options.
- Global patience increases, required to reach later days when that option is enabled.
- Reduce group size items, lowering your starting customer group size over the course of a run.
- Filler items such as patience boosts, customer reductions, and mess reduction.
- Trap items such as patience decreases, extra customers, and group size increases.
