# PlateUp

## Where is the options page?

The [player settings page for this game](../player-settings) contains all the options for configuring your randomizer experience.

## What does randomization do to this game?

PlateUp's randomizer integrates with Archipelago to randomize dish unlocks, day progression, and franchise runs. Day completions and franchise milestones become location checks, while dish unlocks, speed upgrades, money cap increases, and other progression items are shuffled into the multiworld item pool.

## What is the goal of PlateUp?

Depending on your settings, the goal is one of:
- **Complete X Days**: Survive and complete a configurable number of restaurant days.
- **Franchise X Times**: Successfully franchise your restaurant a configurable number of times.
- **Reach Day X With Dishes**: Reach a target day while also having a minimum number of dishes active.

## What are location checks in PlateUp?

Location checks are actions performed in-game that send items to other players in a multiworld game. Checks include:
- Completing each day of a run.
- Earning stars during a run.
- Completing franchise milestones.
- Unlocking and playing specific dishes (if dish checks are enabled).
- Meeting setting-based objectives (if setting checks are enabled).

## What items can I receive?

Items you can receive from other players include:
- Dish unlock items (allowing you to open a restaurant with that dish).
- Speed upgrades for players and appliances.
- Day leases (required to progress past certain days).
- Money cap increases.
- Shop size increases.
- Trap cards (negative effects like patience decreases or extra customers).
