# Running with Rifles

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure
and export a config file.

## What does randomization do to this game?

In the vanilla campaign, your rank determines which weapons, equipment, radio calls, and squad size
you unlock. In the randomized version:

- **Squadmate Slots** (rank-ups) are items in the multiworld pool. Each one increases your rank by 1,
  giving you a bigger squad and access to better gear.
- **Weapons** can be shuffled by category (e.g., "Machineguns") or individually (e.g., "AK-47").
  You can't use a weapon until you've received the corresponding item.
- **Radio calls** are shuffled. You need both the Radio master item and specific call items to use them.
- **Map access keys** gate progression between the 10 campaign maps and 2 final missions.
- **Side missions** are locations that send items to other players.
- **Vanilla grenades, vests, and costumes** can optionally be shuffled into the pool (grouped or individual).

## What is the goal?

Depending on your options:

- **Campaign Complete** (default): Complete both Final Missions.
- **Maps Conquered**: Conquer a configurable number of maps.

## What items can I receive from other worlds?

- Squadmate Slots (rank promotions)
- Map access keys (11 keys, starting map is always unlocked)
- Weapon unlocks (9 categories or ~196 individual weapons)
- Radio call unlocks (12 calls + master Radio item)
- Equipment (vests, deployables, binoculars)
- Throwables (C4, claymores, impact grenades, flares)
- Vanilla grenades, vests, and costumes (if shuffled)
- RP bundles, XP boosts, rare weapon vouchers
- Traps (demotion, radio jammer, friendly fire)
