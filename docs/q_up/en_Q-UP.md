# Q-UP

## what does randomization do to this game?
You start with a random champ. The skills available to you are randomized as well and their positions are swapped.
Additionally, shop upgrade locations and rank up rewards have also been swapped.

## which items can be found in another player's world?
Every randomized item can be found in the multiworld, which includes skills, upgrade points, crystals, shop upgrades,
corruption shards, and gold.

## what does another world's item look like in Q-UP?
Items can be rank and level up rewards. Challenges that can contain multiworld items are marked with the Archipelago 
logo. Multiworld items in the shop include the player's name.

## when the player receives an item, what happens?
The next time you enter the lobby, you will receive a notification for each item you received. The maximum amount of 
unread notifications is nine.
