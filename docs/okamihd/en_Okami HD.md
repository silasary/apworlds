# Okami HD

## Status : In-Development

## What items do get randomized ?

Currently, the items randomized are:
- All Brush Powers and Upgrades
- All Divine Instruments, excepted Divine Retribution *(you always start with it)*.
- Tools and Treasures
- Stray Beads, although they don't have any use yet.

- Story and quest items are Work In Progress.
- Dungeons Items and Praise related upgrades are **not** included yet.*(The first Astral pouch is a static Sphere 0 item to avoid a softlock)*


## What locations do give randomized items ?

- All chests and Brush Gods in Arc 1 (up to Moon Cave Orochi)
- All merchants in Arc 1 can give randomized items.
- Bosses don't give randomized items yet.
