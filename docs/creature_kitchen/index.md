# Creature Kitchen

Creature Kitchen is a cozy cooking game where you feed creatures to earn
keys and explore the house. In Archipelago mode, all keys, recipe cards,
ingredients, and crest halves are shuffled across locations — yours and
other players&#x27; worlds.
