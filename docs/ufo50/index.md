# UFO 50

UFO 50 is a collection of 50 single and multiplayer games from the creators of Spelunky, Downwell, Air Land &amp; Sea,
Skorpulac, Catacomb Kids, and Madhouse.
Jump in and explore a variety of genres, from platformers and shoot &#x27;em ups to puzzle games and RPGs.
Our goal is to combine a familiar 8-bit aesthetic with new ideas and modern game design sensibilities.
