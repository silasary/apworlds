# Super Chipflake Ü: Quest for the Uncooked Schnitzel

## What is Super Chipflake Ü?

Super Chipflake Ü is a collectathon platformer inspired by the classics of the N64 and GameCube era.
Help cats with their silly problems on your quest to eat the world's best Schnitzel 

## What does randomization do to this game?

Right now, the Quests and Stickers as well as the DiceMice's shop are randomized.

## What is the goal of Super Chipflake Ü when randomized?

The goal is the same as vanilla: get all five ingredients and make the schnitzel.


## What does another world's item look like in Super Chipflake Ü?

When getting another world's item, a message like the game's original ones will pop up with the Archipelago Logo.
Shop items are not (yet) replaced.

# Usage
To play, you need:
- The PC version of [Super Chipflake Ü](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3064060/)
- The [latest pre-release build of BepInEx 6 for IL2CPP x64](https://builds.bepinex.dev/projects/bepinex_be) installed into the game
- [The mod itself](https://github.com/Max-G4/Chipflake_AP/releases/tag/releases)

To install BepInEx, just unzip the folder into the game's main directory.

To install the mod, just drag the unzipped Chipflake_AP folder into your Game\BepInEx\plugins folder.

To connect to the server, there is a UI in the top left. Pause the game to show your cursor and to be able to type in the AP server and login details, then click on Connect.
