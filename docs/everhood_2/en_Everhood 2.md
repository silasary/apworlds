## What Does Randomization do to this Game?
- All items giving via cutscenes or chests have been randomized.
- Experience rewards from fights are optionally randomized.
- More to come.


## What is the Goal of Everhood 2 in Archipelago

The goal of Everhood 2 is defeat the dragon. (Or similar goal.) To do this, you'll need to collect Power Gems to unlock the door.

## What Other Adjustments have been made to the Base Game?
- Several areas have been adjusted to allow backtracking.
- Return To Cosmic Hub button was added to the menu.
- More to come.
