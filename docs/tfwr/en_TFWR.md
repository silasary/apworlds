# The Farmer Was Replaced

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure and export a
config file.

## What does randomization do in this game?

It will randomize how upgrades are unlocked.

## Which items can be placed in another player's world?

Upgrades

## What does another world's item look like in The Farmer Was Replaced?

You won't see any items yet. They're unlocked by completing achievements.
Eventually, they may show up on a notification.
