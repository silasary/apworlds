# Castlevania: Portrait of Ruin

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure and export a config file.

## What does randomization do to this game?
As of right now, Portrait of Ruin randomizes most items.

## What is the goal when randomized?
The goal is to complete a set number of Portraits (by defeating the boss of that portrait) and defeating either Brauner or Dracula.
If the Nest of Evil is required, you will need to pick up the item behind the Doppelganger fight for it to register as completed.

## What items and locations get shuffled?
The base location pool consists of all static pickups, as well as the starting quest, Preparations.

## What happens when I receive an item?
When you receive an item, it should automatically be added into your game.
