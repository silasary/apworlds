# Rhythm Doctor

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure and export a config file.

## What does randomization do to this game?

Levels and wards are locked behind their respective items, and clearing levels will not unlock other wards, levels, or night shifts.

Clearing levels will send locations:
- For standard levels, locations are sent out by a level's rank (B, A, and S ranks).
- For boss levels, prioritized locations are sent out by how well you did (Clear, Complete+ without Checkpoints (if applicable), and Perfect ranks).
- For '5-B1 - Rhythm Weightlifter', for the first five stages default locations are sent out, with stages past 6 having excluded locations instead.
- Intermissions ('2-4 - Song of the Sea', '2-4N - Song of the Sea (Night)', '5-3 - Seventh-Inning Stretch') and any '5-B1 - Rhythm Weightlifter' stages past stage 6 are excluded.

If the end goal is 'X-0 - Helping Hands', 'X-1 - Art Exercise' is moved to the basement, and the Garden Room will unlock when all bosses have been cleared instead of being unlocked by a key item.

## What other changes are made to the game?

Rhythm Dogtor is made available as a dedicated level ('3-DOG - Rhythm Dogtor'). It is accessible as the night shift of '3-X - One Shift More'.
