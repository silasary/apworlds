# Shadow of the Colossus

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure and export a
config file.

## What does randomization do to this game?

There are 3 ways to play this game:

Kill All Colossus - based on the options in the yaml, you kill a number of colossus and retrieve their Idol Shards. The Colossi you must fight are randomized, except for the final boss. When you have an idol shard from every other colossus you'll be able to use the gate at F8 and make your way to the final boss. Kill him and the game is complete!

Hunt All Lizards - based on options in the yaml, you can hunt and kill a number of lizards in the game. Collect their tails to clear. The number of lizards is defined in the yaml options. This is recommended for those who just want to relax and chill out. You can completely ignore all colossi kills in this way.

Hunt All Shards - A somewhat more chill hunt, like hunt all lizards. The difference being you can expect shards to be behind colossi you've set in your yaml options. Collect lal the shards to clear!


## What items and locations get shuffled?

- Large Stamina and HP Upgrades from killing the colossi vanilla. These are "progressive".
- Smaller Stamina and HP Upgrades you would normally get from fruit and lizards. These are Junk
- Sigils will be shuffled into the pool as "keys". Each sigil matches a colossi and lets you spawn it.
