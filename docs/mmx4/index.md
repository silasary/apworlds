# Mega Man X4

Mega Man X4 is a 1997 action-platform game developed and published by Capcom. 
Mega Man X4 allows the player to choose between the two mechanoid &quot;Reploids&quot; protagonists at the beginning of the game
X, who uses an arm cannon; or Zero, who wields an energy blade.
Two Maverick Hunters, X and Zero, fight against a Reploid army called the &quot;Repliforce&quot; who are waging war against humanity to earn their independence.
