## What is this game?

Flowersanity is a game about merging flowers and mixing their colors. 
It was made for the Archipelago Game Jam 2026.
By spawning in the flower colors you received and merging them using different calculation modes, 
you create and thereby check new colors.

## What is the goal of Flowersanity in Archipelago?

You can choose between (currently) 2 goals:
1. Colors count: Have a certain number of colors received through either the multiworld or through merging.
2. Specific colors: Have a certain list of colors received through either the multiworld or through merging.

## Which items can be in another player's world?

- Flower colors (~7800 currently)
- Merging calculations (6 currently)

## What is considered a location check?

- Spawning in a flower of a certain color
- Merging flowers into a different one with a certain color
- 6 free checks after connecting to the multiworld

## Disclaimer about the color's names

All the color's names were taken from https://colornames.org/, 
where they're published under the CCO 1.0 license and downloadable as a zipped `.csv` file.
From all the names included in the download, all names with less than 9 votes as of March 17th 2026 
(and a few hundred hand-selected ones) were removed in order to get a final list of ~7800 color names.
