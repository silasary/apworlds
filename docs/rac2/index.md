# Ratchet &amp; Clank 2

Ratchet &amp; Clank 2: Going Commando is a third-person shooter platform game originally for the PlayStation 2. Play as
Ratchet and Clank as they attempt to unravel a conspiracy in a new galaxy involving a mysterious &quot;pet project&quot;
orchestrated by the shadowy MegaCorp.
