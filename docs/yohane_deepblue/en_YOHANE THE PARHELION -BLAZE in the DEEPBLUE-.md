## What does randomization do to this game?
Item which the player would normally collect throughout the game have been moved around and the final boss is now locked behing defeating all 8 
main bosses instead of rescuing all ally characters.

## What items and locations get randomized?
All ally characters, their upgrades and important movement upgrades have been randomized, on top of that, materials with limited amounts 
available have also been added to the item pool. All chests and boss defeats are locations, and rescuing an ally is another check.

## What does another world's item look like in YOHANE THE PARHELION -BLAZE in the DEEPBLUE-?
Any items in the game are unchanged (subject to change).
