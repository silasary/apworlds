# Final Fantasy X

## Things to know:

- Blocked all travel directly between Regions. Trying to enter another region will now either lead to the Airship menu or simply turn you around
- Airship Codes have been disabled.
- If you unlock Seymour, he will only be available in battle and not in the party menu.

## What is randomized?:
The world is split into regions which can be played in any order.

You can receive consumable items, key items, weapons and armor, Celestial items, Party Members, Aeons, Regions, and Weapon/Armor/Aeon Customizations.

## What are the locations?
Treasures (chests, rewards from NPCs), viewing Jecht Spheres, defeating Bosses, recruiting Blitzball players, capturing monsters, 

## What is the goal?
Defeat Yu Yevon.

Depending on options the final battle may be locked until you find all party members, visiting all temples as part of your pilgrimage, or if Capture-Sanity is enabled, defeating Nemesis.

## Known issues:

- You must open CSR _after_ opening the game.
- Succeeding the Jecht Shot scene with CSR will crash the game.
- CSR in gneral is known to be buggy and occasionally unpredictable.

- Sometimes crashes occur, or autosaves place you into a softlock. Manually save often!
