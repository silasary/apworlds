# Ufouria

## What does randomization do to this game?

Items which the player would normally acquire throughout the game have been moved around. Logic remains, so the game is
always able to be completed, but because of the item shuffle, the player may need to access certain areas before they
would in the vanilla game.

## What is the goal of Ufouria when randomized?

Simply defeat the final boss like in the vanilla game. This will require all 3 colored keys, and access to the final boss door, which requires Suction to reach it from the left side or Freeon to reach it from the right side (via the ice corridor's southeast door).

## What items and locations can get shuffled?

Locations in which items can be found:
- All 3 party member boss fights
- All 24 chests

Items that can be shuffled:
- All original items found in the above locations

## What does another world's item look like in Ufouria?

There are no visual changes to received checks.

## When the player receives an item, what happens?

Nothing at the moment, so keep the Bizhawk client open to see when new items come in.
