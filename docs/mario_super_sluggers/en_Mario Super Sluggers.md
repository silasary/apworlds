# Mario Super Sluggers

## Where is the options page?

You can read through all the options and generate a YAML [here](../player-options).

## What does randomization do to this game?

Every character recruit, interactable object, and key item (excluding shops) in Challenge Mode counts as a check and
will send a random item. The ability to change between day and night is also randomized into the pool.

## What is the goal of Mario Super Sluggers when randomized?

In order to beat Mario Super Sluggers in AP, you must unlock the required number of characters as set in your options,
change day to night, then defeat the Bowser Monsters in a game at Bowser Castle in Challenge Mode.
