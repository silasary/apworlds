# Mario Super Sluggers

## Where is the options page?

You can read through all the options and generate a YAML [here](../player-options).

## What does randomization do to this game?

Every character recruit, interactable object, and key item in Challenge Mode counts as a check and will send a random
item. The ability to change between day and night is also randomized into the pool.

## What is the goal of Mario Super Sluggers when randomized?

The goal condition can be chosen from any of the following:
- Defeat Bowser Monsters: Unlock a full team and the day-night cycle, then defeat the Bowser Monsters in a game.
- Play Badge: Unlock and win all minigames (including Toy Field).
- Friend Badge: Recruit all characters.
- Star Badge: Have star status on every character, then defeat the Bowser Monsters in a game.
