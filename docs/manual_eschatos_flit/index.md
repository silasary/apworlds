# Manual_ESCHATOS_Flit

A Manual implementation for ESCHATOS, a vertical shmup originally released for the Xbox 360 and later ported to PC, Switch, and PS4.
Shoot your way through each AREA to stop the invasion of Purple Erosion from beneath the Moon&#x27;s surface!
