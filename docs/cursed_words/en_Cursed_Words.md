# Cursed Words

## Where is the options page?
You can configure your randomization options on the [Cursed Words Player Options](../player-options) page.

## What does randomization do to this game?
In this Cursed Words randomizer, all **Characters**, **Stickers**, **Stamps** and **Tile Types** will be received in a non-standard order:
- Characters will be locked until received from the multiworld
- Stickers and Stamps will not be purchasable in the shop until received from the multiworld.
- Tile Types will not appear in grids until received from the multiworld.

## What is the goal of Cursed Words when randomized?
The currently available goal is to win runs with each selected character that has been set in the Options YAML.
_More goals coming soon..._

## Which items can be in another player's world?
The currently available items for this randomizer are:

- **Characters** -> Playable characters will unlock and be available in the character selection menu.
- **Sticker / Stamp Bundles** -> Sets of Stickers and Stamps will unlock and become purchasable in the shop.
- **Tile Types** -> Types of tiles will unlock and be able to  spawn on grids and in the shop.
- **Sticker / Stamp Slots** -> Sticker and Stamp inventory slots will unlock and be able to hold stickers and stamps.
- **Grid Re-rolls** -> The amount of grid re-rolls per encounter will be increased.
- **Money** -> Additional money, when received during a run.

## What does another world's item look like in Cursed Words?
Items from other worlds do not currently take any visible appearance in-game, but are found by completing objectives such as:
- Winning encounters with each character
- Submitting words with specific tile lengths
- Submitting words with score amounts
- Buying, Selling and Re-Stocking the Shop

In a future update, items will be available to purchase in the shop and will therefore have a visible appearance.

## When the player receives an item, what happens?
Currently, all received items are automatically applied and immedately become available to use/purchase.

## How many items can I find or receive in my world?
Currently, with the first 6 implemented characters, there are up to **118** different Locations and Items available.
_More locations and items planned for future updates..._
