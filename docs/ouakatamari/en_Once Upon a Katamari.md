# Once Upon a Katamari

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure and export a
config file.

## What items get shuffled?
The following items are shuffled into the item pool:
- Stage Unlocks
- Cousins
- Presents
- Planets
- Stardust (filler)

## What locations get shuffled?
The following locations are checks:
- Stage Clears
- Cousins
- Presents
- Crowns

## What is the goal of this game?
You must collect a number of Planets to unlock the final stage "That Hole...". Complete that stage to goal the game.

## What changes are made from vanilla?
In vanilla, only one cousin appears in a stage at a time, and you must play through a stage multiple times in order
to collect all cousins. In Archipelago, all cousins are immediately available on the first playthrough of a stage.  
A setting exists in the mod config for "easyFinale". This setting will mean you only need to roll up one item to reach
the target size in "That Hole...", meaning you don't need to beat enough stages to goal. Logic assumes this setting is
enabled.
