# Door to Door Door Salesman

### Items / Locations
The items are doors, upgrades, and neighborhood unlocks.  
The locations are old doors, shop items, and neighborhood unlocks

### Options
- Deathlink: Toggles [deathlink](https://https://archipelago.miraheze.org/wiki/Death_Link) for the player
- Deathlink Amnesty: The amount of deaths required to send a deathlink

### Goaling
The game ends after buying out every shop item.
