# Final Fantasy XIII-2 Randomiser information

## Gates and Artefacts

Normally in Episode 3 of the base game, the gate links depend on the order in which you complete Sunleth Waterscape 300AF And Yaschas Massif 1XAF. For the randomiser we fix these associations so that Yaschas Massif 1XAF story gate always leads to Serendipity, and Sunleth Waterscape 300AF story gate always leads to the Void Beyond.

The Episode 3 and Episode 5 chaos "chains" are also adjusted. The first Void Beyond visit is always skipped (and can be just passed over on the Historia Crux), and the second will only retain the chaos effect for the second Void Beyond visit and Hollow Seclusion. This means when playing without Historia Crux randomisation, you can freely navigate the crux after Hollow Seclusion before entering A Dying World.

The base game contains 10 Wild Artefacts which can be used to open any of the 10 Wild Gates throughout the game.

For the randomiser, these Wild Artefacts are replaced with specific (custom) Artefact items, each one corresponding to a specific gate. The full list is given below.

Even when the Historia Crux is randomised, Gates still require their "vanilla" outward Artefact. For example, the gate in New Bodhum 3AF still requires the Giant's Artefact, even if that gate takes you to Sunleth Waterscape 300AF.

## Mog Levels

Instead of Moogle abilities (Hunt, Throw, Advanced Hunt) being unlocked at story locations directly, they have been converted into "Progressive Mog Level" items.

## Shop levels

Normally the contents of Chocolina's shop increase as you progress through the game invisibly. For randomisation purposes, these have been adjusted into "Progressive Shop Level" items, that will be granted upon entering an area where a jump would normally take place.

## Area specific changes

This section outlines any changes from the base game to the randomiser experience, outside of the obvious "things will be in different places".

In the below information, if the change requires the base unrandomised location to be obtained, it will be worded as "Some Item Check", whereas if just the item needs to be in your inventory, regardless of where it was found, it will be worded as "Some Item".

### Bresha Ruins 5AF

Opening the locked door in the southwest of Excavation Site is not possible until after interacting with Alyssa and receiving the "Academy Communicator" Check.

### Sunleth Waterscape 300AF

Weakening Mutantomato requires you to have the "Black Hole Gem" and "White Hole Gem" fragments. This can lead to situations where you can defeat the boss before clearing most of the area. If you have these fragments on your initial visit, the normal unweakened intro fight will still play out, to then fight the weakened boss simply leave and re-enter the zone via the Historia Crux.

Currently, the "Black Hole Gem" is not randomised, and will always be granted by defeating Fearyl in Archylte Steppe ???AF

### Augusta Tower 200AF

Progressing through the tower requires the "Access Key 50" item.

### A Dying World 700AF

Entering A Dying World will remove Noel from your party until defeating the boss in the Gogmagog Gamma location. You can return to the Historia Crux during this time, but access to Noel will not be restored until progressing in the area.

Once the Gate Seal is obtained, closing the Gates from this area will cause you to restart the area from the Serah solo section, allowing the paradox ending to be still obtained (in theory) even with Historia Crux randomisation.

### Academia 400AF

Map completion requirements for Academia only now require 4xx/400 access - 500 map completion is not necessary (to account/allow for fixed endgame)

### Academia 4XXAF

Monster professor can now also be obtained after defeating 13 bosses. Each boss grants a Boss Token, and the count is used here.

The boss locations (as the fights may be randomised) included are:
- Aloeidai (Yaschas Massif 10AF)
- Atlas (Weakened version, Bresha Ruins 5AF)
- Caius (Oerba 200AF)
- Caius (Serah Solo fight, Void Beyond)

- Gogmagog Alpha (New Bodhum 3AF)
- Gogmagog Beta (New Bodhum 3AF)

This list may be expanded in the future, if it is, options will also be introduced to adjust the number of bosses required to obtain the fragment

## Known Issues/Limitations

Gate Seals and closing gates has some known issues where it does not work as intended. For now all paradox endings are marked as excluded locations for AP purposes due to this. Re-opening the gate should generally restore any strange state that closing it causes.

The base game has a restriction on only being able to obtain 2 fragment missions in any given area at once, preventing you from starting another until one of the previously accepted missions are complete.

In a randomised situation, this can potentially lead to some softlocks if you access missions before you can complete them and then become unable to start a new mission otherwise.

Some safety latches exist to prevent this in some situations (e.g. Bresha Ruins 100/300).

Similarly, some checks can be missed if a mission is handed in before the chain of items it normally sets up has been checked. Where possible, the scripts have been
adjusted to prevent this, although some situations may still exist. If you run into a situation where a check is no longer accessible please report it!
