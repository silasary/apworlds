# Dark Cloud

## Where is the options page?

There isn't one :O

## What does randomization do to this game?

Atla in dungeons as well as optionally miracle chests in town are randomized.

2 chests in Bunbuku's House are not randomized as circumstances can make them missable.

## What items and locations get shuffled?

Georama pieces and items normally in miracle chests.

## Which items can be in another player's world?

Any of the items which can be shuffled may also be placed into another player's world. It is possible to choose to limit
certain items to your own world.

## What does another world's item look like in Dark Cloud?

No visuals are shown when finding items.

## When the player receives an item, what happens?

If the player is in a dungeon, an in-game message will say what was received.
