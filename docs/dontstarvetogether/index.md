# Don&#x27;t Starve Together

Don&#x27;t Starve Together is a game where you are thrown into a strange and unexplored world full of odd creatures,
hidden dangers, and ancient secrets known as &quot;The Constant&quot;. You must gather resources to craft items and build
structures and farms to help you protect yourself, survive, and most importantly, not starve.
