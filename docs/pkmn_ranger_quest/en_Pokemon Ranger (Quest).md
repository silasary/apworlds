## What is this game?

Pokémon Ranger is a Nintendo DS game from 2006 that revolves around capturing and befriending pokémon 
by drawing many circles around them using the console's touch screen.

## How does this apworld work?

Instead of the usual way of playing a game in Archipelago by starting a new save file, playing through the main story, 
getting stuck due to not having the required items to progress, and finally winning a boss fight, 
this apworld shuffles quests into the multiworld's item pool. 
Once obtained, you can complete such a quest in the game (most of them on an already completed save file), 
which will check the `Complete quest #<X>` location, where `X` is the Xth obtained quest.
The goal is to just complete a chosen number of random quests.
