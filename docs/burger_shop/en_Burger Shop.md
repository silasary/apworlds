# Burger Shop

## What does randomization do to this game?

All recipes, powerups, and stages are included in the item pool. What makes Burger Shop unique is
that a majority of these items are food recipes which make the game more difficult, and will be
labeled as trap items in the item pool.

- **Food items and recipes** — burgers, chicken sandwiches, sides, drinks, sundaes, shakes, snack trays, salad
  ingredients, and condiments are locked until received. Customer orders update in real-time to only include
  unlocked recipes, so customers will never ask for something you haven't found yet.
- **Level access** is gated by **Progressive Burger Shop** keys. The first 10 levels are always accessible;
  each key received unlocks the next 10. Reaching the Alien chapter (levels 71–80) additionally requires the three Alien food items.
- **Workstation**, **BurgerBot**, and **Cookies** are items in
  the pool that must be found before they can be used.
- **Powerups** (Cookie, Speed, Happy, Money) are individual items. Each powerup will be available in all levels once it is unlocked.
- **Progressive Salad** unlocks one additional salad ingredient per copy received, in a fixed order (Lettuce, Tomato, Onion, Olives, Croutons).

## What is the goal when randomized?

Beat the final Story Mode level. This requires
all 7 Progressive Burger Shop keys and the three Alien food items (Triple Bacon Cheeseburger, Ranch Dressing, Large Lemon Lime Soda).

## Which items can be in another player's world?

- **Progressive Burger Shop** (7 copies) — level access keys
- **Food items** — all burger, chicken sandwich, side, drink, sundae, shake, snack tray, and condiment recipes
- **Progressive Salad** (5 copies) — each copy unlocks one more salad ingredient
- **Gameplay upgrades** — Workstation, BurgerBot, Cookies, Cookie Powerup, Speed Powerup, Happy Powerup,
  Money Powerup
- **Filler** — Lucky Penny (and bonus recipes when the Bonus Recipes option is enabled)

## What does another world's item look like in Burger Shop?

The Archipelago client window shows a notification whenever an item is received. Customer orders update
automatically — starting or restarting a level will reflect any newly unlocked recipes. Powerup
changes take effect immediately without requiring a level restart.
