# Mega Man 3

Following his second defeat by Mega Man, Dr. Wily has finally come to his senses. He and Dr. Light begin work on
Gamma, a giant peacekeeping robot. However, Gamma&#x27;s power source, the Energy Elements, are being guarded by the
Robot Masters sent to retrieve them. It&#x27;s up to Mega Man to retrieve the Energy Elements and defeat the mastermind
behind the Robot Masters&#x27; betrayal.
