# 2048

## What is 2048?

2048 is the hit game from 2014.

The goal of the game is to obtain a 2048 by merging equal numbers to make a single one, twice as big.

The interface presented here is minimal: no undo button, no fancy graphics, no sound, just pure gaming.

## How does 2048 rando work?

Your ability to merge things is taken away, you start unable to merge anything, the ability to merge things is shuffled in the pool.

The locations are: getting every power of 2 up to 2048, and getting to some score thresholds.

Do note that getting a number doesn't mean you have to get it through a merge, you are expected to clear the "Have a 2" and "Have a 4" locations without any item.

## Once I'm inside the 2048 client, how do I actually play 2048?

Arrow keys to move all the numbers in the grid, merging numbers if able. There is also a reset button above the grid.
