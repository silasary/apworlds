# Streets of Rage

Streets of Rage (titled Bare Knuckle in Japan) is a series of side-scrolling beat &#x27;em up video games. It centers on the efforts of several ex-police vigilantes trying to rid the fictional American metropolis of Wood Oak City of a crime syndicate that has corrupted its local government.
